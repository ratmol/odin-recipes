Description

One of the world's most sought after dish. Easy to make and so many variations it might get you confused. But fear not of the confusion we are here to help you out to make this dish. Below are the ingredients and steps you need to follow to make it

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta in a pot for 10 to 15 minutes.
  2. Heat a pan put some oil and saute the garlic.
  3. Add a whole can of tomato sauce.
  4. Pour the cooked pasta with salt to your preference.
  5. Turn off the heat and grate cheese over it according to your preference. (The more the better)
  6. Add the basil and pepper.

There you go your easy to make and tasty pasta.
