Description

I am sure you may have had some type of dumpling in your life. They go well with anything and are one of the most tasty and cheap foods in the earth. Every country has their own kind of variations but the Nepali dumpling which has a savory and spicy taste is one of the bes =t you will ever have in your life.

Ingredients

  • Minced meat
  • Wheat flour
  • Momo masala
  • Tomato
  • Cashew
  • Onion
  • Green Chilli
  • Garlic and Ginger

Steps

There are two different steps to create this dish. One is for the dumpling and the other is for the sauce.

Dumpling
  1. Marinate your minced meat by adding the momo masala as well as diced onions, garlic and ginger paste.
  2. Prepare a dough with mixture of wheat flour and water. Let it rest for 30 minutes
  3. **Disclaimer** During the 30 minute break make the sauce.
  4. After the 30 minutes create a thin layer of dough and put some oil on it.
  5. Add the marinated minced meat in the thin layer and carve some designs.
  6. After sufficient dumplings heat the steamer, oil the base and put the dumpling in it.
  7. Heat the steamer in medium heat and wait between 10 to 15 minutes.

Sauce

  1. Boil or fry your tomatoes. About 4 to 5.
  2. Saute your cashew by adding some diced onions, chilli, garlic and ginger with salt and momo masala.
  3. Let everything cool down and place all of it in a mixture
  4. Mix the mixture and there you have your sauce. For extra flavours add schezwan pepper.

Well there you have it. Pour the sauce on top of your dumpling and it's ready to eat.
