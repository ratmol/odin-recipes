Description

If you wonder what's with this name of the chicken it's because if you make this for your date i bet you they will ask you to marry them. Lol!. Enough with the jokes they might not say it but it will definetely leave a great impression on them of you that they will neber forget. This easy to cook recipe is one of my favorite ways to cook a chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Butter
  • Italian seasonings
  • Rosemary leaves
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Heavy cream

Steps

  1. Season your chicken and grill it in the oven at 325 for 25 minutes.
  2. After your chicken is done heat your pan and put 2 tablespoons of butter.
  3. Add onions and garlic and saute them while basking in the butter.
  4. Pour your heavy cream about 150 ml into the pan.
  5. Put your rosemary leave in it and let the aroma kick in.
  6. Now put your cooked chicken in the pan and sear it each side and serve it.

There you have your cooked marry me chicken
